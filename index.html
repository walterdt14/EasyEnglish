My First Week at a New Job

My name is Emily. I am twenty-four years old, and I live in a small apartment with my sister. Last Monday, I started a new job at a hotel.

It is my first time working in a hotel, so I was a little nervous. Before this job, I worked in a small café near my home. I enjoyed my old job, but I wanted to learn new skills and meet new people.

Every morning, I wake up at 6:30 a.m. I take a shower, get dressed, and prepare breakfast. I usually have a cup of coffee, two slices of toast, and a banana.

At 7:30 a.m., I leave my apartment and walk to the bus stop. The bus arrives at 7:40, and it takes about twenty-five minutes to reach the hotel.

My job is at the front desk. I answer the phone, welcome guests, check reservations, and give information about the hotel.

At 12:30 p.m., I have my lunch break. I usually eat a sandwich, some fruit, and drink a bottle of water.

I finish work at 5:00 p.m. Before I leave, I check that everything is ready for the evening receptionist.

I am very happy with my new job. Every day I learn something new, improve my English, and meet interesting people from around the world.
